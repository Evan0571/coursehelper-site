Common help topics

Account access. If you cannot sign in, check your email login code, Apple sign-in status, and network connection, then try again.

Recordings and transcription. Recording and audio processing can require microphone, speech recognition, storage, and network access. Only record or upload class content when you have the required permission.

Subscriptions and credits. Purchases are handled through Apple in-app purchases. Subscription cancellation and many refund requests are managed through your Apple ID purchase settings.

Privacy or deletion requests. You can delete your account in the app settings. You can also email support for privacy, data, or deletion questions.

常见支持问题

账号登录。 如果无法登录，请先检查邮箱验证码、Apple 登录状态和网络连接，然后重试。

录音与转录。 录音和音频处理可能需要麦克风、语音识别、存储和网络权限。只有在获得必要许可后，才录制或上传课堂内容。

订阅与 credits。 购买通过 Apple 应用内购买处理。订阅取消和很多退款请求需要在 Apple ID 的购买设置中管理。

隐私或删除请求。 你可以在 app 设置中删除账号，也可以通过支持邮箱咨询隐私、数据或删除问题。
